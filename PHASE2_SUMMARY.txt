METRICS & CALCULATIONS
================================================================================

STAGNATION DAYS
---------------
Formula: current_date - published_date
Bands:
  - 0-30 days:   Normal (Green)
  - 31-45 days:  Watch (Blue)
  - 46-60 days:  Attention (Yellow)
  - 61-180 days: Critical (Orange)
  - 180+ days:   Urgent (Red)

CVR (Conversion Rate)
---------------------
Formula: (email_inquiries / detail_views) × 100
Thresholds:
  - ≥5%:  Excellent (Green)
  - 2-5%: Good (Blue)
  - <2%:  Needs Improvement (Red)

ROTATION PRIORITY SCORE
------------------------
Formula: (stagnation_days × 1.0) + ((5 - cvr) × 10.0) + (price_changes × 5.0)
Higher score = Higher priority to sell

DISCOUNT FLAG
-------------
Triggered if ANY of:
  - stagnation_days ≥ 60
  - cvr < 2.0 (and cvr > 0)
